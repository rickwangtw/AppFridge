App Fridge

Reinvented App Freezing for Android!

Batch freeze and unfreeze apps

Multi-select apps in App State Perspective and freeze or unfreeze the apps in batch.

You may also group the apps into user-defined groups in App Group Perspective, and freeze or unfreeze them together.

Launch frozen apps

You may launch apps even if it is frozen! Just click on the app! That simple!

  • Android versions later than Ice Cream Sandwich 4.0.3
  • ROOT required for freezing and unfreezing apps!
